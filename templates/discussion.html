What is Gaussian Elimination?

Gaussian Elimination is a numerical method used to solve systems of linear equations by transforming the augmented matrix into upper triangular form.

Partial Pivoting

Partial Pivoting improves numerical stability by swapping rows so the largest pivot element is used.
